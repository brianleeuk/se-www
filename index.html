About Shocking Electric

Shocking Electric is a professional electrical service company based in South West London. We specialize in a wide range of electrical services, including installation, maintenance, and repair of residential and commercial electrical systems. Our team is certified, highly trained, and equipped with the latest tools and technology to ensure safe and reliable results. Whether it’s upgrading electrical panels, wiring new installations, or troubleshooting complex electrical issues, we deliver exceptional workmanship that meets industry standards and regulations. Trust us to provide the highest level of safety, quality, and customer satisfaction for all your electrical needs.
